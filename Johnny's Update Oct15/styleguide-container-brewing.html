Branding Ideas

What dose the company do?

Ever since the revolution of craft beer in Vancouver, numerous breweries pop out to the market Container Brewing is one of the most recent craft beer breweries found in 2019. Other than making beers, they also have their own taproom opening soon to hosts guests who are looking forward to trying fresh draft beer on tap.

Who is the potention customers

Anyone who loves craft beers, except minors. Most likely in the age between 25 to 50

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

Selling beers in various liquor stores, hosting tasting events, having happy hours, having food in taproom, having good graphic design on both beer cans and websites

Color Palettes

Art Direction

Black, dark navy, and gold are chosen to be the color scheme of the webpage
Raleway is chosen to be the font for headers, as it brings out a bold and extragerate feeling
Muli is chosen to be the font for body, as the minimalistic of the font eliminates visual distraction.
